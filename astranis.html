The Solution

Microsat GEO — the right tool for the job.

35,786 km GEO Orbit

Astranis builds small, dedicated geostationary satellites — roughly the size of a washing machine — that operate in GEO orbit to deliver focused broadband coverage over a specific region.

  • Fraction of the cost of a large GEO satellite
  • 2–3 year build and launch timeline (vs. 10–15 yr)
  • Dedicated bandwidth for a single operator/region
  • Rapid iteration — software-defined architecture

05 / 08
How It Works

From contract to broadband in 3 steps.

01
Operator contracts a MicroGEO satellite
An internet service provider or telecom operator in an underserved region purchases a dedicated satellite. Astranis handles design, manufacturing, and launch coordination.
02
Launch & in-orbit delivery
The satellite launches as a rideshare payload (SpaceX Falcon 9, etc.) and maneuvers to its designated GEO slot. Astranis manages on-orbit checkout and hands off to the operator.
03
Operator delivers broadband to end users
The operator runs ground infrastructure and distributes internet service to homes, businesses, and government facilities in the coverage area — often for the first time.
